About Us

Our Mission

Based in the Naperville area, we understand the joys—and challenges—of being a dog owner. As lifelong dog lovers, we know firsthand how easy it is to forget about cleanup until it becomes a problem—whether that’s a ruined lawnmower, dirty patio furniture, or a surprise step from a neighbor’s kid. We believe picking up after your dog shouldn’t be a stressful chore or a barrier for those with limited mobility. That’s why we’re committed to making cleanup simple, convenient, and worry-free for every pet owner. We also give back—10% of our proceeds go to local animal shelters, and we proudly partner with campus clubs to support pet adoption and raise awareness for animal welfare organizations.

Our Service

Just tell us how many dogs you have, your address, and how often you'd like your lawn cleaned—we'll handle the rest! It's simple, reliable, and hassle-free care designed to keep your yard clean and your mind at ease.
