Meet The Founders

As two business students at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with a passion for animals, we seek out every oppourtunity possible to connect with the local community and our furry friends! That’s why we started Squirts—a subscription-based poop pickup service designed to make pet ownership easier, cleaner, and more enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a busy student, a working professional, or a family with a full plate, we're here to help you keep your yard spotless and stress-free.
